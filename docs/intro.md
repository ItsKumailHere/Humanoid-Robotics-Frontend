# Spec-Driven Textbook: Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

## Welcome

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This textbook provides a thorough exploration of the principles, technologies, and applications that define this exciting and rapidly evolving field.

## About This Textbook

This textbook is structured to build your understanding progressively from fundamental concepts to advanced topics:

1. **Chapter 1: Basics** - Introduces the fundamental concepts of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics
2. **Chapter 2: Physical AI** - Explores how artificial intelligence is applied to physical systems
3. **Chapter 3: Humanoid Robotics** - Focuses on the design and mechanics of humanoid robots
4. **Chapter 4: Control Systems** - Examines control systems that enable robotic movement and interaction
5. **Chapter 5: Advanced Topics** - Covers simulation, ethics, and future directions

## Learning Approach

This textbook takes a spec-driven approach to learning, emphasizing:
- Clear learning objectives for each chapter
- Theoretical foundations with practical applications
- Exercises to reinforce understanding
- Further reading for deeper exploration

## Target Audience

This textbook is designed for:
- Undergraduate and graduate students in robotics, AI, and related fields
- Researchers entering the field of Physical AI and humanoid robotics
- Engineers working on robotic systems
- Anyone with a technical background interested in understanding this field

## How to Use This Textbook

- Start with Chapter 1 if you're new to the field
- Use the exercises at the end of each chapter to test your understanding
- Consult the further reading sections for additional resources
- Refer to the appendices for supplementary information
